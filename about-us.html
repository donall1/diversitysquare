The Space Between Available Now!

Troy has just wrapped up his third book, The Space Between, a bittersweet story of a father and a daughter forced to deal with the damning effects of drug addiction on a middle-class family.

Based on a true story, we follow 21-year-old Misha Mahoney, a former straight-A student who lands in a court appointed drug program. A hard-nose judge presides over attendees who must complete a stringent set of requirements or face a year in jail. The one difference in the Juvenile Drug Offender Program, or J-DOP, and other programs is the requirement that a parent participate in the program. Enter alcoholic Todd Mahoney.

Forced to raise his daughter alone after the death of his wife, Todd suddenly finds his life turned upside down by the constant presence of court and recovery, and the ever-watchful scrutiny of a judge who is relentless in his oversight of J-DOP. What before may have been a casual lifestyle of drinking with friends at parties or nights out becomes the constant subject of criticism as Todd navigates the judge's ever-changing playbook.

As Misha succeeds at getting clean and staying out of trouble, she becomes the judge's favorite, creating a sort of love triangle between Misha and a “hang ‘em high” lawman who demands loyalty, and the girl and her father, who wants nothing more than his daughter to find her way. The stress of J-DOP becomes too much for Todd, whose life eventually falls apart. But in finally accepting his own need for recovery, he can step back and aid his daughter in realizing hers.

Ninety Degrees At Midnight
Available Now!

Dottie Stankel just wanted to get pregnant and be a mother. Scott Stankel just wanted to make his wife happy. When both sacrifice everything to serve the other, only the secrets of Dr. Xiang will unearth their true passions and show them the truth about their love.

Author Troy Bastian takes us on a journey through three decades of poems and short stories; early works that not only demonstrate an unbridled and spirited snapshot of life on a quest for something more - the compelling stories within reveal the darker side of human nature, and open our eyes to the consequences our choices will leave behind when we're gone.

Troy compiled more than 20 years of poems and short stories with the freshly told story of the Stankels in Bottles and Diapers, a compilation of work that spans his entire career.

You’ll be mesmerized by the unique characters and unexpected events that are sure to make you question your own beliefs about the world we live in.

Slot Machine Miracles Available Now!

Donnie Canady is a successful TV journalist, a husband, and father of three - he embarks on a journey to tell the story of the homeless who live in the Las Vegas underground. Obsessed with finding the secrets of the Mole People, Donnie finds himself drawn into the dark and mysterious world of the unwanted, the unloved - the hundreds of homeless who have disappeared from the world to battle their demons. But where will Donnie turn when those demons come calling for him?

Troy Bastian spent more than 10 years researching addition and the homeless in Las Vegas for Slot Machine Miracles. The novel follows a TV journalist who attempts to penetrate the underground – a sprawling 200-mile plus system of draining canals where the “mole people” seek refuge from the heat, the police, and their pasts.

The journalist hopes to crank out a huge story, but quickly finds himself drawn into their world of crime, drugs, and addiction – along with an unseen enemy who quickly sets his mark on destroying Donnie.

With a healthy dose of suspense and a host of real-life images from the real tunnel underground, Slot Machine Miracles takes a disturbing look at what happens to human beings when they reach their breaking point. Will Donnie figure out the truth before it breaks him too?

Soon to be an Independent Feature Film!

Troy also has written a screenplay, The Canal, which follows Donnie on his journey to reveal the world of the mole people. The production, slated for late 2022, will take fans of the book even deeper into the dark and mysterious world of the Las Vegas underground.
